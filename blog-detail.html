Following our initial introduction in earlier June, customers from Yangon and Mandalay can now access our new Hybrid Broadband plans; the fastest internet on the fastest growing fiber and wireless broadband network. CEO of TUTU Broadband said, “We’ll continue densifying advanced technology across our footprint throughout 2022 and a part of our future expansion efforts includes covering 50,000 customer households by year-end 2022.”
As part of those expansion efforts, we’ll be bringing Ananda Fiber and TUTU Broadband Fiber to Yangon, Mandalay and to Shan State as well by year-end 2022. Customers in these areas can sign up to be alerted when the services are available to their address by visiting ananda.com.mm
